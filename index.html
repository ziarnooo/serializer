How It Works

01

Record

Open a form. Click the fields you want filled. Type the default values. Serializer remembers every step. Takes about two minutes.

02

Save

Give the template a name. It's stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere.

03

Replay

Next visit, click "Fill form." Fields fill in seconds. Review. Submit when you're ready.
